About Me

Hi, I'm Rohma! I graduated CUNY Brooklyn College in May 2020 with a B.S. in Physics and a minor in Computer Science and Math. While in college I did research in graph theory, superconducters, and gas hydrates (not all at once). I presented the graph theory findings at the NYC Regional Math Alliance Conference. I did a data science internship with a Professor of mine in school, and was secretary of The Computer Science Club, where we planned the first Brooklyn College Hackathon. I interned at a health insurance company for three consecutive summers. While in school I won the Sam Schwartz Award in 2018 and the Melba Phillips Prize in 2019 for achievements in physics. This Spring I was TA-ing for intro to java and I’m currently looking for work in industry. I’m also an ameature baker and an avid reader.
I will try to update my blog with things people find interesting! And you can visit my github to see random projects of mine or my linkedin!
