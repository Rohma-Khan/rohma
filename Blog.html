Introduction to Quantum Cryptography

Title description, May 13, 2020

Brief introduction to quantum cryptography. This was written for my Quantum Mechanics 2 course. I had taken Cryptography and Cryptanalysis the semester before and was at the time sitting in on Introduction to Hardware & Algorithms of Quantum Computing. This paper was kind of a breeze because I picked a topic that I was already well-versed in and did a very shallow overview.

Supercapacitors: Electrode and Electrolyte Materials

May 19, 2020

This was written for a special topics in physics course I took, called Materials for Energy Conversion and Storage. Initially the class was almost all in lab work. I was to build different supercapacitors that would be used as part of a larger experiment. Unfortunately due to Covid the class switched to remote learning about halfway through the semester. I never finished making the capacitors. This presentation was given to the other students in the class who were focusing on other materials.
